Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions.

How long does it take to build a website?

Starter Business websites take 3–5 days.

Business websites take 7–10 days.

Premium websites take 14–21 days, depending on complexity.

Do I need to buy a domain and hosting?

Yes, I can guide you step by step on where to buy an affordable domain and hosting, and assist with the setup. If you prefer, I can manage everything for you, and we can discuss pricing based on your needs.

Can I request changes?

Yes. Up to 2 revision rounds are included during the design stage to ensure everything meets your expectations.

What if I don't like the first design?

No problem. I’ll refine the layout, colors, or sections within the included revision rounds to make sure it meets your expectations.

How does payment work?

Payment terms:
100% upfront for Starter Business websites.
50% upfront / 50% on completion for business and premium projects.

Do you provide support after launch?

Yes. I provide 30 days of free support after launch and can help with small updates or guidance when needed.
